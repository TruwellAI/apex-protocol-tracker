# Testosterone Delivery Methods — Research Reference
## For Apex Protocol Tracker Calculator

### Key Apex Pen Selling Point
"One pen for your peptides AND your testosterone — simplify your protocol."
SubQ testosterone with a dial-a-dose pen is already practiced clinically.

### Dosing Formula
`pen_units = (desired_mg / concentration_mg_per_ml) * 100`

### Example: Test Cyp 200mg/mL, 140mg/week, 2x/week
- Per injection: 70mg
- Volume: 0.35mL
- Pen units: 35 units

### Testosterone Cypionate
- Concentrations: 100, 200, 250 mg/mL
- Vial sizes: 1mL, 5mL, 10mL
- Carrier oils: Cottonseed (brand), Grapeseed (compounding)
- Half-life: ~8 days
- Dose ranges: Low 60-80mg/wk, Mid 100-150mg/wk, High 160-200mg/wk
- SubQ compatible: YES — pen compatible
- Frequencies: daily, EOD, 3x/wk, 2x/wk, weekly

### Testosterone Enanthate
- Concentrations: 200, 250 mg/mL
- Carrier oils: Sesame (brand), Grapeseed (compounding)
- Half-life: ~7-8 days (same as cyp clinically)
- Same dose ranges as cypionate
- SubQ compatible: YES — pen compatible

### Testosterone Propionate
- Concentration: 100 mg/mL
- Carrier oils: Grapeseed, MCT
- Half-life: ~2-3 days (SHORT)
- Dose ranges: Low 50-75mg/wk, Mid 75-100mg/wk, High 100-150mg/wk
- Requires EOD or daily injection
- SubQ compatible: YES — ideal for pen due to small daily volumes

### Testosterone Cream
- Strengths: 10% (100mg/mL), 20% (200mg/mL)
- Application: scrotal (highest absorption), forearms, shoulders
- Daily dose: 10-40mg applied topically
- NOT pen compatible
- Transfer risk to partners/children

### Testosterone Pellets (Testopel)
- 75mg per pellet, 6-12 pellets per insertion
- Every 3-6 months
- Surgically implanted
- NOT pen compatible
- Cannot adjust dose once placed

### SubQ Injection Notes
- 27-31 gauge needle, 5/16" to 1/2" length
- Max comfortable subQ volume: 0.3-0.5mL per site
- Oil-based = slightly more viscosity, warming helps
- Injection sites: abdominal fat, lateral thigh, love handles
