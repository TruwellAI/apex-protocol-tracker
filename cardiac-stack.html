01 / WHY THIS STACK

Four Peptides. Four Cardiac Levers.

Cardiac disease is rarely one thing. It's a layered failure: mitochondria stop producing clean ATP, inflammation drives fibrosis, damaged tissue can't repair, and metabolic dysregulation accelerates everything. No single peptide addresses all four. This stack does.

  • SS-31 rebuilds the engine — stabilizes cardiolipin on the inner mitochondrial membrane where cardiac cells generate 70% of their ATP
  • MOTS-c tunes the fuel system — improves mitochondrial fuel efficiency and insulin sensitivity (both cardioprotective)
  • BPC-157 reduces cardiac fibrosis — NO/VEGF pathway + anti-fibrotic preclinical data, gently hypotensive
  • TB-500 drives cardiac tissue repair — FDA orphan drug designation for post-MI recovery, angiogenesis, cardiomyocyte survival

Each peptide hits a different mechanism. Zero redundancy. Zero copper (eliminating the KLOW/GLOW degradation problem). Clean chemistry, compatible reconstitution.

02 / THE FOUR PEPTIDES

What Each One Does

SS-31 (Elamipretide)
Primary Cardiac · Mitochondrial Rebuilder
1,000 mcg
DAILY · AM
Mechanism: Cardiolipin-targeting tetrapeptide. Binds to the inner mitochondrial membrane and stabilizes cristae structure. As you age (or post-MI), cardiolipin gets oxidized and mitochondria become leaky, producing less ATP and more ROS. SS-31 reverses this damage at the most fundamental level.

Cardiac evidence: FDA Fast Track designation for Heart Failure with Preserved Ejection Fraction (HFpEF), Barth syndrome (genetic cardiomyopathy), and mitochondrial cardiomyopathy. Multiple active Phase 2/Phase 3 trials. Multiple cardiac studies on PubMed →

Why AM: Cardiac mitochondrial protein turnover peaks with daytime metabolic demand.
MOTS-c
Mitochondrial + Metabolic Adjunct
500 mcg
DAILY · AM FASTED
Mechanism: Mitochondrial-encoded peptide. Activates AMPK. Improves insulin sensitivity and metabolic flexibility. Works upstream of SS-31 — tunes how efficiently your mitochondria burn fuel before SS-31 protects their structure.

Cardiac relevance: Insulin resistance is a major cardiac-disease driver. Metabolic flexibility improvements reduce lipotoxic cardiac damage. Preclinical cardiac preservation data in aged animal models.

Why AM fasted: Peak AMPK activation response when insulin is low.
BPC-157
Anti-Fibrotic · Cardioprotective
500 mcg
DAILY · BEDTIME
Mechanism: NO/VEGF pathway activation, angiogenesis, anti-fibrotic signaling. In preclinical cardiac models, BPC-157 reduces cardiac fibrosis, protects against ischemic damage, and modulates arrhythmia. Mildly hypotensive — good for most cardiac patients, monitor if already hypotensive.

Cardiac evidence: Multiple animal models demonstrating cardioprotective effects. Rodent studies show reduced infarct size, improved post-MI function, anti-arrhythmic properties. Human data limited but growing.

Why bedtime: Pairs with overnight tissue repair cycles. Also separates from morning mitochondrial peptides (cleaner metabolic handoff).
TB-500 (Thymosin Beta-4)
Post-MI Recovery · Systemic Repair
1 mg
2×/WEEK · Mon + Thu
Mechanism: Actin sequestration, cell migration, angiogenesis. Critical role in cardiac repair after ischemic damage — promotes cardiomyocyte survival, upregulates VEGF in damaged myocardium, drives new capillary growth.

Cardiac evidence: FDA Orphan Drug designation for post-MI recovery. Clinical trials have explored post-MI use. Direct cardiac-regeneration animal data. Probably the most-studied peptide for actual cardiac repair.

Why 2×/week: Long half-life (~2-3 days), cumulative systemic effect. Mon + Thu spacing = ~3.5 day intervals for continuous coverage. Bedtime dosing fine (systemic reach takes 18–24 hrs).
03 / DAILY SCHEDULE

Two Syringes Per Day

Because of the AM/PM timing split, this stack uses 2 separate injections daily. On Monday and Thursday evenings, TB-500 co-mixes with BPC-157 into one evening syringe (compatible chemistry, no copper).

🌅 MORNING (AM) · SYRINGE 1
SS-31 + MOTS-c co-drawn into one syringe
• SS-31 1,000 mcg (daily)
• MOTS-c 500 mcg (daily, fasted)
Same syringe · SubQ · belly fat · alternate sites
🌙 BEDTIME · SYRINGE 2
BPC-157 daily + TB-500 on Mon + Thu
• BPC-157 500 mcg (daily)
• TB-500 1 mg (Mon + Thu only — co-mix with BPC on those nights)
Same syringe on Mon/Thu · BPC solo on other nights

All four peptides are chemistry-compatible. No copper = no oxidation concern. The only split is timing + frequency.

04 / RECONSTITUTION

Four Separate Vials

Each peptide ships as its own 10 mg lyophilized vial. Reconstitute each separately with bacteriostatic water.

  • SS-31 10 mg + 3 mL BAC = 3,333 mcg/mL → 33.3 mcg per unit → dose = 30 units
  • MOTS-c 10 mg + 3 mL BAC = 3,333 mcg/mL → 33.3 mcg per unit → dose = 15 units
  • BPC-157 10 mg + 3 mL BAC = 3,333 mcg/mL → 33.3 mcg per unit → dose = 15 units
  • TB-500 10 mg + 3 mL BAC = 3,333 mcg/mL → 33.3 mcg per unit → dose = 30 units (2×/week)

Shelf life: 21 days refrigerated for each peptide (shortest = SS-31 class limit).

Co-mix in one syringe (AM): Draw 30 units SS-31 first, then draw 15 units MOTS-c into the same syringe. Total = 45 units in one injection.

Co-mix in one syringe (PM Mon/Thu): Draw 15 units BPC-157 first, then 30 units TB-500 into the same syringe. Total = 45 units.

PM other nights: Just 15 units BPC-157 alone.

05 / MONITORING (CARDIOLOGIST-DIRECTED)

Required Labs and Monitoring

This is NOT optional. These peptides affect cardiac function, BP, and hematocrit. Required monitoring:

Baseline (before starting)

  • Echocardiogram with ejection fraction + diastolic function
  • 12-lead ECG
  • CBC with hematocrit
  • Comprehensive metabolic panel
  • Lipid panel + hs-CRP
  • BNP or NT-proBNP
  • Medication reconciliation with cardiologist
  • Blood pressure baseline (at home, 7-day average)

Every 4 weeks during the cycle

  • Home BP (log twice daily)
  • CBC with hematocrit (watch for >52%)
  • Symptom review (fatigue, SOB, palpitations, edema)

End of 8-week cycle

  • Repeat echocardiogram
  • Repeat ECG
  • Repeat BNP
  • Repeat lipid panel
  • Decision with cardiologist: continue, adjust, or discontinue
06 / CYCLE PROTOCOL

8 Weeks On / 4 Weeks Off

Run the full stack for 8 weeks. Then take 4 weeks off to avoid tolerance (especially to SS-31 mitochondrial saturation effects). During the break, basic cardiac nutrition support (CoQ10, magnesium glycinate, taurine, omega-3) continues.

Typical program: 3 cycles per year with 4-week breaks between each.
