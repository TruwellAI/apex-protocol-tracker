Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026
Quick summary: Apex Protocol is an education platform and protocol tracker. We store the minimum data needed to make the app work. Most of your data never leaves your device. We do not sell or trade your information. We do not show you ads.

1. Who we are

Apex Protocol ("Apex," "we," "us," "our") operates the Apex Protocol web application (apexdosing.com) and the Apex Protocol mobile applications on iOS and Android (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding it.

Apex Protocol is provided for research and educational purposes only. It is not a healthcare service, is not subject to HIPAA, and does not provide medical advice.

2. Information we collect

Data you provide directly

  • Email address — optional, only if you create an account for cloud sync. You can use the Service anonymously without an email.
  • Protocol preferences — peptides you've added to your stack, dose levels, timing, reminder times. Stored on your device in local storage. Synced to our servers only if you opt into cloud sync.
  • Subscription status — if you purchase a premium subscription, we receive confirmation from Apple or Google that your subscription is active. We do not receive your payment card details.

Data collected automatically

  • Usage data — anonymized metrics (which screens you open, how long you use the app). Used to improve the product. Not linked to your identity.
  • Device data — device model, operating system version, app version. Used for crash diagnostics and compatibility.
  • Crash logs — automatically captured by Apple/Google when the app crashes. No personal content is included.

Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your real name, address, phone number, or payment card details.
  • We do not collect health records, lab results, or any data regulated by HIPAA.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not share or sell any data with advertising networks.
  • We do not use Facebook Pixel, Google Ads tracking, or any cross-site advertising trackers.

3. How we use your information

We use the limited data we collect to:

  • Operate the app — show you your stack, send dose reminders, track your cycle progress
  • Sync your data across your devices (only if you opt in to cloud sync)
  • Verify your subscription status with Apple/Google billing
  • Fix bugs and crashes (anonymized diagnostic data)
  • Improve the product based on aggregate usage patterns

We do not use your data to build advertising profiles, sell to third parties, or share with insurance companies, employers, or medical entities.

  • Apple App Store — app distribution and subscription billing (iOS users). Governed by Apple's privacy policy.

  • Netlify — web hosting and backend functions. Data transit is encrypted.

None of these third parties sell your data for advertising.

6. Your rights over your data

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — see everything we have about you
  • Correct your data — update anything that's wrong
  • Delete your account and all associated data — full account deletion, no questions asked
  • Export your data — download a JSON or PDF copy of your stack and history
  • Opt out of analytics — you can disable anonymized usage analytics in the app's settings
  • Opt out of cloud sync — keep all your data local to your device only

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@apexdosing.com. We'll respond within 30 days.

California residents (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete it, and the right to opt out of any sale of personal information. Apex does not sell personal information.

European residents (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation including the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or port your personal data. Our legal basis for processing is (a) your consent when you create an account or opt into features, and (b) legitimate interest in operating and improving the Service.

7. Children's privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 17 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 17. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us at privacy@apexdosing.com and we will delete it promptly.

8. Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we permanently delete your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (e.g., billing records for tax purposes).

Anonymized usage analytics are retained indefinitely in aggregate form but cannot be linked back to you.

9. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS), encryption at rest for cloud-synced data, and secure API authentication. No system is 100% secure — we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we continuously work to protect your information.

If we ever experience a data breach that affects your information, we will notify you within 72 hours as required by applicable law.

10. International data transfers

Our servers are located in the United States. If you are using the Service from outside the US, your data will be transferred to and processed in the US. By using the Service you consent to this transfer.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you via the app or email (if you have provided one). Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
